# Introduction

Welcome to **Artisan Sale Manager** - the most powerful and user-friendly WooCommerce sale management plugin available.

## What is Artisan Sale Manager?

Artisan Sale Manager transforms how you handle sales and promotions in your WooCommerce store. Instead of manually updating products one by one, you can create sophisticated sale campaigns that manage thousands of products with just a few clicks.
